Community Guidelines

Welcome to our online library community! We strive to provide a positive and inclusive environment for all users. To ensure a smooth and enjoyable experience for everyone, we kindly ask you to adhere to the following guidelines:

Respect Others

Respect fellow community members and library staff. Avoid offensive language, harassment, and discrimination based on race, gender, religion, nationality, or any other personal characteristics.

Contribute Constructively

Participate in discussions and interactions in a constructive manner. Provide helpful and relevant contributions that contribute to the community's knowledge and understanding.

Protect Privacy

Respect the privacy of others. Do not share personal information about yourself or others without their consent. Be cautious when sharing information online.

Follow Copyright Laws

Adhere to copyright laws and respect intellectual property rights. Do not distribute or share copyrighted material without proper authorization.

Report Violations

If you encounter any violations of these guidelines or observe inappropriate behavior, please report it to the library staff immediately. We rely on the community to help maintain a safe and welcoming environment.

Consequences of Violations

Violations of these guidelines may result in disciplinary action, including warnings, suspension, or banning from the library platform. Repeat offenders may face permanent expulsion.

Thank you for your cooperation in creating a positive and inclusive community!
